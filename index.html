About Us

EClimate Actions EClimate Actions is a climate-focused Non-Governmental Organisation based in Cross River State, Nigeria. We are committed to promoting renewable energy, climate education and community-driven environmental sustainability. We empower communities to take practical climate actions that reduce carbon emissions and strengthen resilience to climate change impacts.

Founded by Ebri, Joshua I., To empower communities to combat climate change by promoting clean energy solutions, climate awareness and sustainable environmental practices that secure a healthier future for all.

Our Services

Climate & Environmental Projects

Tree planting, waste management, environmental restoration, community outreach.

Renewable Energy (Solar)

Solar installation, training, maintenance and energy consultancy services.

Capacity Training

Digital skills, climate education, leadership and entrepreneurship development.

Projects & Impact

We have implemented environmental education programs, tree planting campaigns, training initiatives and clean energy projects across communities in Nigeria.
